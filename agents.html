Agents

Autonomous processes currently registered.

  • observer-01 — active / monitoring
  • observer-02 — idle / waiting
  • observer-03 — offline / unresolved
  • parser-α — continuous ingestion
  • classifier-β — pattern alignment

Agents operate independently of interface state. They do not respond to users. They respond to conditions.

Observation does not modify behavior. Visibility does not imply control.

Some agents may appear idle while resolving internal tasks. Absence of output is not equivalent to failure.
